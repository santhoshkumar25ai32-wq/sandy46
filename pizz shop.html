BMW

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke) is a famous German luxury car manufacturer. The company makes luxury, sports, and electric cars known for performance, comfort, and advanced technology

BMW M3

  1. Engine: 3.0-liter Twin-Turbo Inline-6

  2. Power: Around 473–503 horsepower (depending on the model)

  3. 0–100 km/h: About 3.5 – 4.1 seconds

  4. Transmission: 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic

  5. Drive Type: Rear-wheel drive or xDrive (All-wheel drive)

  6. more

BMW M5

  1. Engine: 4.4-liter Twin-Turbo V8

  2. Power: 617 horsepower (625 PS)

  3. Torque: 750 Nm

  4. 0–100 km/h: About 3.3 seconds

  5. Top Speed: Up to 305 km/h (with M Driver’s Package)

  6. Transmission: 8-speed automatic

  7. Drive System: M xDrive (All-Wheel Drive)

  8. more

BMW i7

  1. Power: up to 544 hp (i7 xDrive60)

  2. Battery: about 101.7 kWh lithium-ion battery

  3. Range: about 560–625 km per charge (WLTP)

  4. 0–100 km/h: around 4.7 seconds

  5. Drive: Electric all-wheel drive (xDrive) with dual motors

  6. more

BMW X5

  1. Engine options: 3.0-liter turbo petrol / diesel

  2. Power: Around 340 hp (varies by variant)

  3. 0–100 km/h: About 5.5 seconds

  4. Transmission: 8-speed automatic

  5. Drive System: xDrive All-Wheel Drive

  6. more
